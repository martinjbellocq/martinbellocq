About

The work behind the numbers.

15 years at the intersection of finance, digital marketing, and growth in Latin America. The foundation: economics and a Master's in Finance, combined with five years at Google advising the marketing teams of hundreds of companies — across insurance, retail, telco, and automotive — on how to compete and grow in the digital world.

The application: eight years as CMO at Ualá, leading market entry in three countries, co-owning commercial results across all revenue lines, and building the brand, team, and growth engine of one of the region's leading digital banks — from 14 employees to 1,400, from zero to 10M+ users. The focus has always been the same: building things that last and growing businesses with real results.

Experience

Brands, teams, and markets — built from zero.

2017 — 2025
Ualá
Chief Marketing Officer

Joined pre-launch in 2017, when fintech in Latin America was still an idea most people didn't trust. The challenge wasn't just building a brand — it was building a category and owning the commercial results that came with it. As part of the Executive Committee, I co-owned revenue performance across four business units: Lending, Investments, Payments, and Merchant Acquiring. Led end-to-end market entry in Argentina, Mexico, and Colombia — from strategy and team building to brand launch and growth execution, including building local teams on the ground in each market and leading Mexico's launch during the COVID-19 pandemic. Over eight years, the company grew from 14 people to 1,400, the marketing organization from zero to 80, and the user base from zero to 10M+. As of 2025, available for new engagements.

2012 — 2017
Google
Senior Account Manager · Account Manager SMB

Five years advising the marketing teams of hundreds of companies — insurance, retail, telco, automotive, education — on how to grow in the digital world. Working at Google also meant experiencing a world-class organization firsthand: global processes, cutting-edge technology, and a culture built around performance. That foundation made the contrast that followed all the more formative — moving to Ualá meant crossing to the other side of the table, with a fraction of the resources and ten times the urgency. Both environments shaped the way I work: structured thinking, startup execution.

2012
American Express
Business Planning Analyst

Financial planning and commercial performance analysis within Merchant Services. A brief but formative stint inside one of the world's most recognized financial brands.

2010 — 2011
Deloitte
Auditor · Barcelona

Financial audits for multinational and regional companies across consumer goods, pharma, and construction. The discipline of reading businesses from the inside out.
